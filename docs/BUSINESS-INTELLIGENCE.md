#### 1. **Regulatory Requirements by State**

**What It Is:**  
Debt management companies must comply with state-specific licensing, bonding, and registration requirements. Requirements vary significantly:
- **Bonding:** Surety bond amounts range from $25K (AZ) to $100K+ (CA, NY)
- **Licensing:** Some states require debt management company license (e.g., CA, FL, TX)
- **Registration:** Many states require annual registration + renewal fees
- **Exemptions:** Some states exempt non-profit credit counseling agencies

**Why It Matters:**  
- Operating without proper licensing = **criminal offense** in many states
- Clients in non-licensed states = **unenforceable contracts**
- State AG enforcement actions common (see: debt relief industry crackdowns 2010-2015)

**Regulatory Requirement:**  
- **Federal:** FTC Telemarketing Sales Rule (advance fee ban)
